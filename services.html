Our Process

How Our Coaching Works

We follow a proven process to ensure you get the results you're looking for

1

Initial Consultation

We start with a comprehensive consultation to understand your goals, history, preferences, and limitations. This helps us create a truly personalized plan.

2

Custom Plan Creation

Based on your consultation, we develop a customized plan that includes workouts, nutrition guidance, and lifestyle recommendations tailored to your specific needs.

3

Implementation & Coaching

You'll work directly with your coach to implement your plan. We provide hands-on guidance, form correction, and ongoing support throughout your journey.

4

Progress Tracking

We regularly track your progress using various metrics beyond just weight, including measurements, performance indicators, energy levels, and photos.

5

Adjustments & Optimization

Based on your progress and feedback, we continuously refine your plan to ensure you're always moving toward your goals efficiently and effectively.

Frequently Asked Questions

The frequency of your coaching sessions depends on the plan you choose. Our Basic plan includes 2 sessions per week, Premium includes 3 sessions per week, and Elite includes 4 sessions per week. However, we can customize the frequency based on your specific needs and schedule.

Not at all! Our coaching programs are designed for individuals at all fitness levels, from complete beginners to advanced athletes. Our coaches will assess your current fitness level and create a program that's appropriate and challenging for you, with proper progressions as you improve.

Yes! We offer both in-person and virtual coaching options. Our online coaching includes custom workout programs, video form checks, nutrition guidance, and regular check-ins through our coaching app. Virtual coaching is a great option for clients who travel frequently, live outside our service area, or prefer to work out on their own schedule.

Our nutrition coaching is completely customized to accommodate your dietary restrictions, preferences, and lifestyle.
