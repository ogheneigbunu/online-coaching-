Common Questions

Booking FAQs

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our booking process

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to ensure you get your preferred time slot. However, we sometimes have same-day availability, so it's always worth checking the calendar if you need a last-minute session.

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before your scheduled session without any charge. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the session cost.

For your first session, please arrive 10-15 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork. Wear comfortable workout clothes and athletic shoes. Bring a water bottle, a small towel, and any relevant medical information or fitness history that might be helpful for your coach.

Yes, you can request a specific coach when booking your session. Each coach has their own calendar of availability, which you can view by selecting their name from the dropdown menu in the booking system.

For first-time clients, we require a credit card to hold your appointment, but you won't be charged until after your session. For returning clients, you can choose to pay at the time of booking or after your session. We accept all major credit cards, cash, and digital payment methods.
